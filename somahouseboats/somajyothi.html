Somajyothi

Our newest addition to the exclusive line of Soma Houseboats, Soma Jyothi is a Heritage Luxuary Houseboat. Nothing beats the novel designs and Soma Jyothi is hailed as the homecoming of the original houseboat design. We went to great lengths to recreate the authentic outside looks while also incorporating utmost comfort in the plush interiors.







You will be torn between your desire to just bask in the plush comfort of interiors or to savour the striking backwater views. Crafted entirely in local and eco- friendly materials ranging from the wood planks and bamboo mats, Soma Jyothi is a master piece of native craftsmanship.


Another Dimension to Your Cruise in The Shimmering Backwaters of Alleppey.

Somatheeram Group has received accolades for our responsible tourism practises. You can indulge in grandeur with the knowledge that Soma Jyothi is fitted with solar power and energy saving equipments. Incorporating a hybrid energy system, Soma Jyothi interiors are beautifully spacious. You will find nothing stuffy inside the vast boat and the amenities are just what you dream about. You get the best of both worlds in this slice of paradise sailing through the middle of nowhere.
The quaint design and languid grace adds another dimension to her beauty. Pukka Kerala cuisine with sea food specialties and local vegetables are prepared fresh on board with high standards by an experienced cook. Peaceful get away to an alternate universe is what Soma Jyothi cruise offers you. Fit for royalty indeed.

Soma Houseboats is a successful venture of the Somatheeram Ayurveda Group, with over 25 years in the industry.

Unique to the land of Kerala, backwaters are a long intricate network of lagoons, lakes, canals, estuaries and deltas of several rivers. A cruise here is a slice of life at its best.
