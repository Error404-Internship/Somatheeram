About Us

A stellar experience like no other, Somatheeram Ayurveda Group brings to you a line of heritage luxury houseboats. Soma Jyothi, our newest houseboat is a wonder like no other.

Kerala Traditional Houseboats

Bringing to you the best of both worlds, our houseboats boast of luxurious interiors with the charm of rustic looks. Unique attraction to the state, Kerala backwaters is a chain of brackish lagoons and lakes lying parallel to the Arabian Sea coast. Our houseboats are manned by an efficient crew and a chef and we stop at nothing to deliver you a delightful tour of the backwaters. And the bottom line? We are committed to ecology and incorporate it in everything we do!

SOMAJYOTHI

Our newest addition to the exclusive line of Soma Houseboats, Soma Jyothi is a Heritage Luxuary Houseboat. Nothing beats the novel designs and Soma Jyothi is hailed as the homecoming of the original houseboat design. We went to great lengths to recreate the authentic outside looks while also incorporating utmost comfort in the plush interiors.
